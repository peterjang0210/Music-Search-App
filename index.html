Muugle,
the Music Search Engine for Free Music Streaming

Muugle is a free streaming platform which allows users to log into their spotify accounts and directly create and stream playlists from the site, all while being able to view their chosen song's information and lyrics.

Get started by searching for a song or artist. From there you will go to the Songlist and click to play whichever song you desire. You also have the options of adding and deleting songs from your playlists, along with creating new ones and accessing them.

You can view the recently played content and the artist/lyric information below.
